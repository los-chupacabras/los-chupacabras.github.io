Electronic Sports, or Esports, is a type of competition focused on multiplayer video games. Esports has been growing greatly in recent years, with an overall fan base of over 400 million people, spanning many different games. League of Legends has one of the biggest esports scenes in the world, known as the LCS, or the League of Legends Championship Series.
